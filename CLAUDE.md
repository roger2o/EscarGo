# EscarGO!

## Concept
A casual mobile-first puzzle game starring a speedy snail. The comedic premise: a snail that goes fast.

## Core Mechanic
- Navigate a snail through compact grid-based arenas
- The snail leaves a slime trail that blocks your path (like Snake meets Tron)
- Collect all targets to complete each level
- Plan your path carefully - your own trail is your biggest obstacle

## Genre & Feel
- Solo puzzle game, level-based with 1-3 star ratings
- Simple mechanic, short sessions, constant progression
- Inspired by: Block Blast, Numberzilla, Candy Crush (casual/addictive loop), Snake, Tron (trail mechanic)

## Star Ratings
- Based on move efficiency (fewer moves = more stars)
- Speed/efficiency humor ties into the snail-going-fast comedy

## Character & Personality
- Cute snail with humor and personality
- Idle animations, celebrations, nervous expressions when the board gets tight
- Racing stripes / motion blur aesthetic
- Rainbow/sparkly trail on 3-star runs

## Themed Worlds (~20 levels each, new mechanic per world)
1. **Garden** - tutorial world, flowers to collect, dirt paths
2. **Kitchen** - crumbs to collect, avoid the chef's footsteps
3. **Park** - rain puddles, leaves, benches as obstacles
4. **City** - tiny snail navigating a sidewalk, collecting crumbs
5. **Space** - snail in a helmet, zero-gravity twist

## Level Mechanics (layered complexity)
1. Basic movement and collection
2. One-way gates
3. Teleporters
4. Breakable walls (pass through once, then solid)
5. Ice tiles (slide until hitting something)
6. Keys & doors
7. Moving obstacles (shift each time you move)
8. Multiple snails (switch between them)

## Tech Stack
- **Web-based PWA** (mobile-first, installable, offline-capable)
- **Phaser** game framework with HTML Canvas
- **TypeScript**
- No app store required - share via link
